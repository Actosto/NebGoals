About

You can create goals with a specific cost and pay them through the donate button. As payments are received, the progress of your goals will grow so you can track your goals.


In addition to your goals, other users ' goals are also displayed. You can support their goals as well as your own with the donate button. In turn, they can support your goals.

Payments

Each donation goes directly to the wallet number from which the goal was created. The donation is free (you pay only for the GAS).
